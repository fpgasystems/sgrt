## sgutil enable vitis

sgutil enable vitis [flags] [--help]

  &nbsp; &nbsp; Enables Vitis SDK (Software Development Kit) and Vitis_HLS (High-Level Synthesis).


### Flags
-v, --version 

  &nbsp; &nbsp; Vitis version.


### Examples
* **$ sgutil enable vitis**
* **$ sgutil enable vitis --version 2022.1**
